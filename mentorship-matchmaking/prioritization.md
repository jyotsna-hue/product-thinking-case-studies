# Feature Prioritization

## Objective

Identify which features should be included in the **initial MVP** of the mentorship discovery platform.

The goal is to prioritize features that **solve the core discovery problem quickly** while minimizing development complexity.

## Features Considered

During the solution exploration phase, several potential features were identified:

- Mentor discovery feed
- Swipe-based interaction
- Advanced search filters
- In-app messaging
- Mentor availability scheduling
- Mentor rating system

However, not all features are required for the **first version of the product**.

To determine the MVP scope, two prioritization frameworks were used:

- RICE Framework
- Impact vs Effort Matrix

---

# RICE Framework

RICE helps evaluate features based on four factors:

- **Reach** – How many users the feature affects
- **Impact** – How much it improves the user experience
- **Confidence** – Certainty of assumptions
- **Effort** – Development complexity

### RICE Evaluation

| Feature | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | Priority |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| Mentor discovery feed | High | High | Medium | Medium | High |
| Swipe interaction | Medium | Medium | Medium | Low | High |
| Advanced search filters | Medium | Medium | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| In-app messaging | Medium | High | Low | High | Medium |
| Mentor scheduling | Low | Medium | Low | High | Low |
| Mentor rating system | Low | Low | Low | Medium | Low |

---

# Impact vs Effort Matrix

To further validate prioritization, features were also mapped based on **impact and implementation effort**.

### High Impact / Low Effort
Features to prioritize for the MVP.

- Mentor discovery feed
- Swipe interaction

### High Impact / High Effort
Important but should be added later.

- In-app messaging

### Medium Impact / Medium Effort
Can be added after the core product proves useful.

- Advanced search filters

### Low Impact
Not required for early product versions.

- Mentor rating system
- Scheduling system

---

# MVP Feature Set

Based on the prioritization analysis, the **initial MVP will focus on solving the discovery problem**.

The MVP will include:

- Mentor discovery feed
- Swipe-based mentor exploration
- Basic mentor profile information
- Mentorship request functionality

More complex features such as messaging, scheduling, and ratings will be explored **after validating engagement with the discovery flow**.

---

# Key Insight

The core friction students face is **discovering the right mentors**.

Therefore, the MVP prioritizes **mentor discovery and lightweight interaction**, rather than building a full mentorship management platform.
