# Feature Prioritization

## Objective

Identify which features should be included in the initial MVP of the mindful spending platform.

The goal is to prioritize features that improve spending awareness while keeping implementation complexity manageable.

---

## Features Considered

During the solution exploration phase, several potential features were identified:

- Micro-spend alerts
- Pause-before-payment prompts
- AI financial assistant
- Spending analytics dashboard
- Subscription tracking
- Investment recommendations

---

## Prioritization Framework

Two frameworks were used:

- RICE Framework
- Impact vs Effort Matrix

---

## RICE Evaluation

| Feature | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | Priority |
|------|------|------|------|------|------|
| Micro-spend alerts | High | High | Medium | Low | High |
| Pause-before-payment prompts | Medium | High | Medium | Low | High |
| AI financial assistant | Medium | Medium | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| Spending analytics dashboard | Medium | Medium | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| Investment recommendations | Low | Medium | Low | High | Low |

---

## MVP Feature Set

The MVP will include:

- Micro-spend alerts
- Pause-before-payment prompts
- AI financial assistant
- Spending insights dashboard

More advanced financial tools can be introduced after validating user engagement.

---

## Key Insight

The MVP focuses on improving spending awareness rather than building a full financial management platform.
