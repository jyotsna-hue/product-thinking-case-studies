# Problem Validation

## Objective

Validate whether users experience difficulty tracking small digital payments and whether they would benefit from spending awareness tools.

---

## Method

An exploratory survey was conducted to understand:

- Digital payment usage patterns
- Typical transaction values
- Difficulty tracking frequent small payments
- Interest in financial insights and AI assistance

---

## Key Questions

Participants were asked:

- Which digital payment method do you use most often?
- What is the typical value of your UPI transactions?
- Do small payments feel harder to track compared to large expenses?
- Which spending insights would you find most useful?
- How comfortable would you be using an AI assistant for financial insights?

---

## Key Observations

Survey responses revealed several patterns:

- Most users rely heavily on UPI for everyday transactions
- Many transactions fall within small-value ranges
- Users often find frequent micro-payments difficult to track
- Many participants expressed interest in AI-assisted financial insights

---

## Insight

The core issue is not payment accessibility but spending awareness.

Users want better visibility into their financial behavior without adding friction to the payment process.
