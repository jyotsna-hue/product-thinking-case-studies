# Problem Discovery

Digital payments have made spending extremely frictionless.

While this convenience improves transaction speed, it also makes spending behavior less visible to users.

Small and frequent UPI payments are particularly difficult to track, which can lead to unnoticed cumulative spending.

Many financial tools today focus on post-spend analysis, showing reports after money has already been spent.

This raises an important question:

How might we help users become aware of their spending before completing transactions?
